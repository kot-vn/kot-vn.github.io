Zeal Education Manager Project

  • Client Aptech Computer Education
  • Category Web Design
  • Date 01/06/2022

The Zeal Education Manager is expected to be built to fulfill the needs of the company / institution known as "Zeal Institute" that provides training. The goal is to create an intranet-based, cost-effective training candidate database management system that includes a variety of day-to-day operations such as: Counseling for a variety of courses; follow-up on previous inquiries; firm advertisements to recruit new applicants for training; complete management of student data, batch details, faculty information, subject, and material information, among other things. Aside from that, it will also take care of the organization's day-to-day operations, such as the number of hours spent by candidates, candidate comments and suggestions, and system maintenance for the company's employees and candidates.
